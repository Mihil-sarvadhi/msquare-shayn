Dashboard Layout Restructuring

Two layout proposals — based on what a D2C brand manager needs first

First-principles logic: A D2C jewelry brand manager opens this dashboard to answer 3 questions in order: (1) How much are we making? → Revenue + Orders KPIs (2) Is our marketing working? → ROAS, Ad Spend, Campaign performance (3) Are orders being fulfilled cleanly? → Logistics, RTO, COD split Reviews and product insights are reference data — important but not urgent on arrival.
